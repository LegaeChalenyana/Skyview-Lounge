Who We Are

Our Story

Founded 2012

Twelve Years of Elevating the Night

Skyview Lounge was founded by Chef Naledi Kgomotso and entrepreneur David Setlhare with a shared vision: Gaborone deserved a dining experience that matched the ambition and energy of its people. What started as a 40-seat rooftop restaurant has grown into one of southern Africa's most celebrated dining and social destinations.

Every choice — from the Portuguese tableware to the custom cedar bar top — reflects an obsessive attention to detail. We source ingredients from within a 200km radius where possible, partner with local artists to rotate the gallery wall pieces, and train every team member in the philosophy that hospitality is not a service but an art form.

In 2019 we were voted Botswana's Best Fine Dining Experience at the African Hospitality Awards. In 2023, our Head Mixologist, Tiro Moleleki, placed second at the Pan-African Cocktail Championship. We are proud of these accolades, but we are even more proud of our regulars who come back, month after month, for the sense of belonging Skyview creates.

What Drives Us

Our Core Philosophy

Seasonal & Local

Our menus change with the seasons. We work directly with Botswana farmers and regional suppliers to ensure the freshest, most responsibly sourced ingredients.

Uncompromising Quality

From the cut of the steak to the thread-count of the table linen, every element is chosen with a singular commitment to excellence.

Genuine Hospitality

We train our team to anticipate needs before they are voiced. Every guest is treated as a guest in our home — valued, comfortable, and remembered.
