Our Story

A Sanctuary Above the City

Founded in 2012, Skyview Lounge was born from a single vision: to create a space where exceptional food, artful mixology, and the energy of the city skyline converge into one seamless experience.

Perched on the 24th floor, our space is curated for those who appreciate the finer details — from the soft glow of our Edison lighting to the hand-selected tableware imported from Portugal.

The Experience

Everything You Need for a Perfect Evening

Fine Dining

Seasonally curated dishes crafted by our Executive Chef, drawing from local Botswana produce and international culinary traditions.

Craft Cocktails

Our master mixologists blend premium spirits with hand-pressed botanicals, creating cocktails that are as visual as they are delicious.

Panoramic Views

Floor-to-ceiling glass panels offer sweeping vistas of the cityscape — a breathtaking backdrop for any occasion.

Live Entertainment

From smooth jazz on weekday evenings to curated DJ sets on weekends, the atmosphere is always perfectly tuned.

Private Events

Curated Wine List
